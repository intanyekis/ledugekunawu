Is Brad Pitt's Girlfriend Ines de Ramon Still Married To Paul Wesley?

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Highlights

  • Brad Pitt is dating Ines de Ramon while navigating a custody battle with Angelina Jolie. They have kept their relationship private.
  • Ines de Ramon was previously married to Paul Wesley, but they announced their separation in September 202
  • Their divorce status is unclear.
  • Brad Pitt and Ines de Ramon met in late 2022 and have enjoyed spending time together. However, Pitt has not introduced her to his children with Jolie yet.

Brad Pitt has reportedly been going steady with his girlfriend, Ines de Ramon, amid his ongoing "biased" custody battle with Angelina Jolie. The couple has succeeded in keeping their relationship private despite both of their previous high-profile marriages.

De Ramon was married to the Vampire Diaries alum Paul Wesley who just filed for divorce in February 2023. There were even rumors that Pitt caused their split. The Babylon actor was first linked to de Ramon in late 2022. Here's what really happened between the three.

What Happened Between Paul Wesley And Ines de Ramon?

Wesley and de Ramon, who got married in 2019, announced their separation back in September 2022. "The decision to separate is mutual and occurred five months ago. They request privacy at this time," their representative told People at that time. It wasn't until November when de Ramon and Pitt were first rumored to be dating.

The Fallen actor officially filed for divorce, five months later, citing irreconcilable differences. De Ramon reportedly filed for divorce the same day Wesley did. But as of this writing, there has been no confirmation on whether Wesley and de Ramon's divorce has been finalized yet.

Wesley was previously married to his Killer Movie co-star, Torrey DeVitto from 2011 to 2013. Before de Ramon, he also dated Phoebe Tonkin. The 41-year-old TV star has now moved on with 23-year-old model, Natalie Kuckenburg who looks a lot like Wesley's ex-wives.

"Just googled natalie kuckenburg and Paul's now ex-wife...does anything else think they both slightly resemble Pauls first wife Torrey Devitto?!" someone wrote on Reddit. Another user replied: "Seems like he has a type.Phoebe also looks like she could pass as Torrey's sister."

How Did Brad Pitt Meet Ines de Ramon?

Pitt and de Ramon were first photographed together backstage at a Bono concert in November 2022. They were seen meeting other A-listers like Cindy Crawford and her husband Rande Gerber, as well as Sean Penn. At that time, a source told People that the two had been seeing each other "for a few months" and that a mutual friend introduced them to each other.

The insider added that de Ramon "is very nice" while Pitt was "really into her." However, they noted that they were just having fun. "It's not an exclusive relationship," said the anonymous tipster. "Ines is cute, fun and energetic. She has a great personality. Brad enjoys spending time with her."

On Valentine's Day this year — possibly days before she and Wesley filed for divorce — Pitt sent the jewelry designer a large bouquet of pink peonies. They also had a romantic date night in Paris ten days later. In July 2023, someone close to the couple said that they're "going very strong."

"They truly enjoy each other's company and are very into each other," they told People. "It's apparent to anyone who sees them together."

Has Ines de Ramon Met Brad Pitt's Kids With Angelina Jolie?

Pitt has been linked to a number of women since his split from Jolie. However, none of them ever met his kids. "He's dated a few women over the years but hasn't introduced any of his children to them," a source told Us Weekly early in October 2023.

That includes de Ramon despite them spending "almost all their free time together" and her becoming "very close" with the Fight Club star's "inner circle of friends." The insider then clarified that while "things between them are going great," Pitt still hasn't introduced his kids to Ines."

They clarified, "It's not that he doesn't love Ines, it's just that he wants to ensure this relationship is going the distance before he takes that major step." Well, we'll have to see if they'll make it to their first anniversary next month. But so far, the two have been each other's great confidantes during their own divorce battles.

"Brad has been really supportive of Ines while she's going through her divorce because he understands how it can be," someone told the magazine in February. "Although Brad knows Ines is completely independent and can handle things on her own, he's let her know that she can always open up to him about anything."

In July, another source said, "It's widely known that Brad is dealing with a lot of legal drama but Ines's feelings for him haven't wavered whatsoever. If anything, it's only made their connection stronger in a lot of ways because they're very open and honest when it comes to communication about their lives, whether it be the good or the bad times."

Meanwhile, there are reports that Jolie has been interfering with Pitt's reconciliation with their eldest son Maddox. He also has a complicated relationship with the rest of his children with whom he only hangs out privately at home.
