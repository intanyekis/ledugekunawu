Lauren Conrad Launches an Affordable Swimwear Line: Pics

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Lauren Conrad‘s fashion empire is always expanding. On Wednesday, May 31, the Hills alum-turned-designer, who is expecting her first child with husband William Tell, launched a gorgeous swimwear line as part of her LC Lauren Conrad brand. “The collection has all the swimsuits, coverups and beachy outfits you’ll need to get ready for summer,” Conrad, 31, promised in an Instagram statement. The super affordable and chic collection is available at Kohl’s.
