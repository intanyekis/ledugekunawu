Mara Hautea Schnittka, best known by her stage name Julia Montes (born March 19, 1995 in Pandacan, Manila, Philippines), is a Filipino-German actress and commercial model. Crowned by ABS-CBN as Royal Prinsesa ng Drama (Royal Drama Princess), she is widely known for her leading roles in Philippine television dramas and movies, such as Mara Clara (2010), as Clara del Valle; Walang Hanggan (2012), as Katerina Alcantara; Muling Buksan Ang Puso (2013), as Sarah Beltran; Ikaw Lamang (2014), as Mona Hidalgo; and in the films, A Moment in Time (2013), as Jillian Linden; and Halik sa Hangin (2015), as Mia Generoso. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
