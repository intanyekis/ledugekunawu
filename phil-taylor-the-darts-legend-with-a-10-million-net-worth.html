Furthermore, Taylor’s influence extends beyond his prowess in the sport itself. Up until 1993, he competed under the banner of the British Darts Organization. This organization would later evolve into the Professional Darts Corporation (PDC), playing a pivotal role in the popularization of darts as a mainstream sport.

Career of Phil Taylor

Phil Taylor’s professional darts career spanned from 1987 to 2018, an era during which he set numerous records and clinched countless titles. He claimed victory in 216 professional tournaments, which included a staggering 85 major titles. Taylor’s crowning achievement remains his remarkable 16 World Championships, an unsurpassed accomplishment in the history of darts.
