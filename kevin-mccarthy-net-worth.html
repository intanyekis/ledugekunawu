Who Is Kevin McCarthy?

Kevin McCarthy Wiki:- Kevin McCarthy was born on January 26, 1965, in Bakersfield, California, U.S. Kevin McCarthy is an American politician. From January to October 2023, he presided as the 55th speaker of the US House of Representatives. He presently represents California's 20th congressional district, which includes the majority of the San Joaquin Valley, as a Republican. From 2002 through 2006, when he was first elected to the U.S. House, he was a member of the California State Assembly.

From 2009 to 2011, McCarthy was the chief deputy whip for the House Republicans, and from 2011 to 2014, he was the majority whip. McCarthy was chosen as majority leader by Speaker John Boehner in 2014 after House Majority Leader Eric Cantor lost his bid for reelection in the Republican primary. McCarthy held onto his post as speaker while Paul Ryan was in office. After Ryan retired in 2019, McCarthy was chosen to lead the house's minority.
