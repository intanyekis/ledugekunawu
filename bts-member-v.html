According to Reports, BTS Member V Will Represent Celine Brand

Publish date: 2024-08-24

V is said to be the most recent member of BTS to sign a contract with a luxury company, following in the footsteps of his comrades. The 27-year-old, whose real name is Kim Tae-Hyung, appears to have joined BLACKPINK’s Lisa and South Korean actor Park Bo-gum as CELINE’s newest brand ambassador.

Before this, the three celebrities went together to Hedi Slimane’s Summer 2023 menswear show, sparking a social media sensation. Les Echos, a French publication owned by CELINE’s parent firm LVMH, remarked on the influence of K-pop in fashion in an article about Paris Fashion Week Men’s even though the news wasn’t official at the time of writing.

BTS Member V

Kim Tae-hyung, also known as V professionally, is a South Korean singer and a member of the boy band BTS. He was born in Korea on December 30, 1995. V has released three solo songs under their name after making his group debut in 2013: “Stigma” in 2016, “Singularity” in 2018, and “Inner Child” in 2020.

These songs reached the top 10 on South Korea’s Gaon Digital Chart. In 2016, he appeared on the soundtrack for the television show Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth, and in 2019, he self-penned and published “Scenery,” his debut independent single.

V was raised in Geochang County and was born Kim Tae-hyung on December 30, 1995, in Daegu’s Seo District. He has a younger brother and sister and is the oldest of three kids. In elementary school, V had the dream of being a professional singer.

He is studying for a Master of Business Administration in Advertising and Media at Hanyang Cyber University in 2021.
