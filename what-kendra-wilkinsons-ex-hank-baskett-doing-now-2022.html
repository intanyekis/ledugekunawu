What Kendra Wilkinson's Ex Hank Baskett Is Really Doing Now

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Model Kendra Wilkinson rose to fame as one of Hugh Hefner's girlfriends thanks to the reality television show The Girls Next Door which she starred in alongside Hefner's other girlfriends Holly Madison and Bridget Marquardt. Wilkinson left the show in 2009 as she decided to start a new life with American football player Hank Baskett.

From what he has been doing since he retired from American football, to what his relationship with his children and Kendra Wilkinson is today — keep on scrolling to find out!

Where Is Hank Baskett Now?

After getting divorced from Kendra Wilkinson, Hank Baskett decided to lead a more private life which was certainly a change from what his fans were used to. The American football player retired in 2011 and unlike most players in his profession — Baskett decided to not pursue a career in analyzing games on television, instead, he became a partner for HBCustoms, a video game console company.

Anyone who follows the former athlete on Instagram knows that his feed mostly consists of sponsored posts in which he is sharing the brand's most recent products. According to HBCustom's website, the brand makes customized PCs that use "the latest technologies with the highest level of customization and attention to detail." As of writing, Hank Baskett has over 277,000 followers on the popular photo-sharing social media platform.

Hank Baskett Has Two Children With Kendra Wilkinson

Hank Baskett and Kendra Wilkinson got married on June 27, 2009, at the Playboy Mansion. Together, Baskett and Wilkinson have a son, Henry Randall "Hank" Baskett IV who was born December 11, 2009, and a daughter Alijah Mary Baskett who was born May 16, 2014. During their relationship, the two were at the center of two reality television shows — Kendra which aired from 2009 until 2011, as well as Kendra on Top which aired from 2012 until 2017. Unfortunately, Baskett and Wilkinson's relationship didn't last and the couple got divorced in 2018. Apart from sharing sponsored content on his Instagram profile, Han Baskett also frequently posts photos of his children. As of writing, the former athlete lives in Voorhees Township, New Jersey.

In an interview with People, Kendra Wilkinson admitted that there is no bad blood between the former spouses. "We both are not perfect humans," Wilkinson said. "Maybe we were just too young. And that's okay. I still love Hank. He's such a good person. But sometimes it just comes to an end." The reality television star and model admitted that co-parenting hasn't been easy to get used to. "It was a rough start for me to get used to it," she admitted. "I didn't know how to not have my kids. I wanted to cry when I wasn't with them."

It seems as if the two stars couldn't work things out, but they are capable of appreciating the good times they shared. "Hank is an amazing person, and I'm so lucky to have been married to him," Wilkinson said. "He is the greatest father. And to be honest, it's going to be hard to find another man like him because the bar was set so high."

"Her and Hank have been co-parenting really well together throughout [the health crisis] and have been for a while now," the source said. "She has her weeks on and off with the kids, and she’s just happy her kids are healthy and in a great place."
