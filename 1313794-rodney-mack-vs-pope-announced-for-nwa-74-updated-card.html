Rodney Mack vs. Pope Announced For NWA 74, Updated Card

Publish date: 2024-07-02

The National Wrestling Alliance has announced a new match for NWA 74.

In a tweet on August 15, the NWA announced that Pope will face Rodney Mack in a singles match.

At the NWA, we love a prize fight and some of the #NWAFam have been clamoring to see @DaBlackPope & @RDoggRodneyMack step in the ring together.

Now we get it.

At #NWA74, these talented fighters go to war!

Will @AronsThoughts have anything to say about who wins? pic.twitter.com/JVWWuP0eLX

— NWA (@nwa) August 15, 2022

The updated cards are as follows:

  • : Kamille (c) vs. Taya Valkyrie
  • : Bully Ray vs. Mike Knox
  • : Homicide (c) vs. Kerry Morton
  • : Jax Dane (c) vs. Cyon
  •  – Winner Receives NWA Women’s Championship Match On Night 2
  • EC3 vs. Mims
  • Luke Hawx vs. VSK
  • Pope vs. Rodney Mack (with Aron Stevens)
  • : Trevor Murdoch (c) vs. Tyrus
  • : Kamille (c) vs. winner of Burke Invitational
  • NWA United States Tag Team Championships 10-Team Battle Royale
  • : Pretty Empowered (Kenzie Paige & Ella Envy) (c) vs. The Hex (Allysin Kay & Marti Belle)
  • MLW National Openweight Championship: Davey Richards (c) vs. Thrillbilly Silas
  • Thom Latimer vs. Judais
  • Caprice Coleman vs. Colby Corino
