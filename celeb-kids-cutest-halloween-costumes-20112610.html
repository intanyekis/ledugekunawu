Celebrity Kids' Cutest Halloween Costumes: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Celebrity kids have shown off their Halloween spirit through years by dressing up in a variety of looks — from adorable to scary to fierce costumes.

From North West dressing up as dad Kanye West‘s Dropout Bear mascot in 2023 to Kylie Jenner’s daughter, Stormi, donning a mini version of her 2019 Met Gala gown, stars’ children have gone above and beyond for the spooky holiday.

In 2023, Khloé Kardashian matched with son Tatum in adorable cow pajama costumes. At the time, Kardashian beamed in cow print makeup, a charcoal tank top and a fuzzy jumpsuit tied at her waist. Tatum, meanwhile, looked adorable in a onesie finished with a tail and ears.

Last year, Clueless star Donald Faison’s daughter Wilder dressed up as his onscreen girlfriend, Dionne. At the time, she wore a black statement hat just as the character did, a black and white plaid set and a tweed purse.
