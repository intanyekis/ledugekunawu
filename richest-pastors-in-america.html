Jesse Duplantis: $20 Million

Jesse Duplantis is an American televangelist, author, and speaker.

Duplantis is the founder and leader of Jesse Duplantis Ministries, a non-profit organization based in Destrehan, Louisiana. Jesse Duplantis is known for his charismatic preaching style and emphasis on prosperity theology.

His teachings often focus on the belief that God desires financial abundance for believers and that faith can unlock material blessings. Duplantis's sermons typically support his message with great humor and biblical references.

He has amassed a fortune worth $20 million, enabling him to live lavishly and travel in style on his private jet.

Rick Warren: $25 Million

Rick Warren and his wife founded the Saddleback Church in the early 1980s.

Rick Warren gained widespread recognition through his book The Purpose Driven Life, published in 2002. Saddleback Church is known for its emphasis on community, small groups, and serving others.

Rick Warren is also known for his philanthropy; he supports several charitable groups, including The PEACE and the Saddleback Emergency Relief Fund.

Despite being one of the wealthiest preachers, Warren has endeavored to live a humble life.

Creflo Dollar: $27 Million

Creflo Dollar is an American televangelist, pastor, and author.

Creflo is the founder and senior pastor of World Changers Church International, a megachurch based in College Park.

The church's teachings often focus on prosperity theology, emphasizing that financial blessings are part of God's plan for believers.

Among the pricey possessions owned by Creflo are a 53-acre mansion, a 182.9-acre country residence, and a Rolls-Royce Phantom.

Creflo also owns a $65 million Gulfstream G650, which is the epitome of luxurious private travel.

Andy Stanley: $45 Million

Andy Stanley was born in Atlanta, Georgia, on May 16, 1958.

He is the senior pastor of North Point Community Church, a mega church located in Alpharetta, Georgia, which he founded in 1995.

North Point Community Church is known for its contemporary worship style. He is recognized for his practical and thought-provoking sermons, often focusing on topics relevant to everyday life and addressing current issues.

Stanley is known for his clear and concise communication style, using storytelling, humor, and illustrations to convey his messages during sermons.

Steven Furtick Jr.: $60 Million

Steven Furtick Jr. is an American pastor, author, and speaker.

Furtick is known for his contemporary worship style, engaging sermons, and focus on reaching the "unchurched," and those who feel disconnected from traditional religious institutions.

He is well-known among younger worshippers for his upbeat delivery and youth-friendly storytelling.

Furtick is worth $60 million, which has enabled him to live a lavish life.

Pat Robertson: $100 Million

Pat Robertson was a television magnate, televangelist, and former Southern Baptist clergyman from the United States.

He owned the Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN) and hosted the television program The 700 Club, which began airing in 1966.

Robertson was a prominent figure in the evangelical Christian community, and significantly shaped conservative Christian politics in the United States.

In addition to being active in several political and social concerns, he utilized his position to promote traditional values; Robertson died in June at the age of 91.

Joel Osteen is an American pastor, televangelist, and author.

Osteen spoke of the period before he took out the loan, recalling a time when he was young and overwhelmed by the prospect of renovating Lakewood Church: "I got the bill back for the bids, and it was $100 million to renovate. And I thought, kind of, God, I didn't sign up for this. I was 36 years old. And I thought, but you know what, God gives you grace for every season of your life."

Kenneth Copeland: $300 Million

Kenneth Copeland is an American televangelist, author, and speaker. He founded Kenneth Copeland Ministries (KCM) in Fort Worth, Texas.

He is known for his charismatic preaching style and emphasis on the prosperity gospel, which teaches that a financial breakthrough is a sign of God's favor and that believers can claim wealth and success through faith.

He has accumulated his vast wealth through writing books, speaking at conferences, hosting radio shows, and undertaking outreach projects.

Kenneth Copeland is renowned for leading a wealthy lifestyle and for owning an expensive private jet.
