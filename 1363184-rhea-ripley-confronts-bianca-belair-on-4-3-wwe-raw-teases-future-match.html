Rhea Ripley Confronts Bianca Belair On 4/3 WWE RAW, Teases Future Match

Publish date: 2024-09-07

WWE SmackDown Women’s Champion Rhea Ripley teased her intent to face WWE RAW Women’s Champion Bianca Belair during the April 3 episode of WWE RAW.

On Monday night, Belair came down to celebrate her recent title defense at WrestleMania 39. As she was praising Asuka for being such a great opponent, though, the new WWE SmackDown Women’s Champion made her way down to the ring. Ripley said Belair wasn’t the “EST,” but was instead the luckiest woman in WWE, because Ripley didn’t choose to face her at WrestleMania.

Ripley then climbed into the ring and got in Belair’s face. “The Eradicator” said she promises that one day, they’ll get back to facing each other. Belair answered the challenged by noting that Ripley chose Flair for a reason, and that whenever she’s ready, they can see who the best of the best is.
