Who is Bonnie Chapman?

Bonnie Chapman is an American producer, makeup artist, and TV personality.

She is known for Dog the Bounty Hunter and Dog and Beth: On the Hunt.

Bonnie Chapman- Bio, Age, Birth, Family, Ethnicity

Bonnie Chapman was born Bonnie Joanne Chapman on Dec 16, 1998, in Denver, Colorado, USA.

Her father is Duane “Dog” Chapman and her mother was Beth Chapman, who died in 2019 due to cancer.

She has a Caucasian ethnicity. Her brother is Garry Chapman.

She is a half-sister of Leland Chapman, Duane Lee Chapman Jr., Lyssa Chapman, James Chapman, Nicholas Chapman, Barbara-Katie Chapman, Wesley Chapman, Tucker Chapman, and Cecily Chapman Barmore.

Education

She has been educated at Sacred Hearts Academy in Hawaii.

Bonnie Chapman: Health

In Oct 2019, Bonnie Chapman was hospitalized but the reason was not disclosed.

Later after she recovered, on her Instagram stories, she posted a post captioning finally home.

She posted a photo of her wrist with a hospital bracelet.

Bonnie Chapman’s Career, Professional Life

Bonnie Chapman is popular in the shows like Dog the Bounty Hunter and Dog and Beth: On the Hunt. She is a producer of these shows.

Dog the Bounty Hunter

It is a reality TV show which was aired by the A&E network. The show premiered in 2001 and ended in 2012.

This show has a total of eight seasons and 246 episodes. In this, the Chapman family used to track and capture the wanted fugitives.

Dog and Beth: On the Hunt

This is another reality show where the Chapman family improves the bail bondsmen’s business techniques and apprehends criminals.

It was aired from 2013 to 2015 by the CMT channel. The show ran for three seasons with 47 episodes.
