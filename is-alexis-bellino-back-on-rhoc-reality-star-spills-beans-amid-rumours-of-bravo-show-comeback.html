Is Alexis Bellino back on RHOC? Reality star spills beans amid rumours of Bravo show comeback

Publish date: 2024-06-13

ORANGE COUNTY, CALIFORNIA: The anticipation of Alexis Bellino's potential return to the new season of 'Real Housewives of Orange County' alongside her new beau John Janssen had been creating quite a buzz. As rumors circulated, hinting at Bellino's full-time comeback to the show and plans for her and Janssen to start shooting in January, she swiftly addressed the speculations, denying the rumors.

Taking to social media to set the record straight in a firm and clear statement, she declared, "THIS IS NOT TRUE!!!!" putting an end to the swirling rumors and clarifying that she won't be joining the 'RHOC' cast.

Earlier Emily Simpson told PageSix that she would love to see Alexis make a comeback to the show. 

“I’m sorry, but bring Alexis back,” she said, adding, “and let Shannon spiral out of control.” "Alexis and I have been friends for years. I love Alexis,” she said, stating that she would “be fine” with her return to the reality show. “I think it would be great.”

However, the mother of three has now refuted all claims of a potential comeback to the show as a full-timer.

Does ‘RHOC’ alum Alexis Bellino have children? 

Alexis has three children which she shares with her ex-partner Jim Bellino.

In a heartfelt revelation in 2022, Alexis shared that her son Miles, born during her previous marriage with Jim Bellino, bravely came out as transgender.

The Missouri native, who celebrated her engagement to her then-fiance Andy Bohn in December 2020, opened up about the journey of understanding and acceptance within their family.

Miles, has two siblings, twin sister Mackenna and brother James.

Why was Alexis Bellino fired from ‘RHOC’?

In 2013, Alexis Bellino left 'Real Housewives of Orange County' after four seasons, with sources suggesting she was fired, as she had reached the end of her storyline.

While Housewives like Tamra Judge and Shannon Beador secured recurring roles, Bravo seemingly aimed to part ways with Alexis even before the last season began filming.
