Who is DPR Live?

DPR Live is a South Korean rapper and singer named Hong Da-bin. His group name DPR stands for Dream Perfect Regime.

Some of his top singles include “know me,” God Bless” and “Right here Right Now”.

DPR Live: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Education

Hong Da Bin was born on January 1, 1993, in Pocheon, South Korea. He was raised in the Guam district.

The artist has not revealed any information about his parents’ names and professions. The rapper holds South Korean nationality and his ethnicity is Asian.

Hong Da Bin wanted to pursue a higher education in Psychology and philosophy but later changed his mind after he attended his mandatory military service.
