Who is Tara Palmeri?

Tara Palmeri is a White House correspondent for POLITICO and a CNN Political Analyst. Prior to the political team, she served as a reporter for POLITICO’s European edition where she covered the Paris and Brussels terror attacks.

In addition, she has also worked as an on-air CNBC contributor.

Tara Palmeri Career, Net Worth, and Awards

Tara began her career as a News Assistant in the D.C. Bureau at CNN in 2009. Later, she moved to the Washington Examiner and co-wrote a daily column called “Yeas & Nays.”

In 2010, she joined the New York Post to write for the paper’s Page Six gossip column. After working as a writer at the New York Post in 2012, Tara began to work as a general assignment reporter.

Furthermore, she has also covered some major news such as the Newtown Connecticut massacre, and the 2012 Democratic National and Republican National conventions followed by the Jovan Belcher murder-suicide.
