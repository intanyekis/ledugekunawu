Lindsay Lohan Jokes About Untreatable Virus, Wears Thong Underwear

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Lindsay Lohan may not be feeling well, but she still wants to look good. The Life Travelers actress stripped down to a barely there Calvin Klein thong on Saturday, Jan. 24.

"#mycalvins are helping me fight off my chikungunya hehe," the 28-year-old captioned an Instagram bathroom pic. The Mean Girls alum wore nothing but the underwear and a long-sleeve crop top, which showed off her very slim midsection and toned legs.
