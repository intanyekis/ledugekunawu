WWE Live Events Results (8/28): Ft. Myers, FL; Tyler Breeze on the Main Roster, Seth Rollins vs. Joh

Publish date: 2024-05-21

The following results are from last night’s WWE live event in Ft. Myers, Florida:

1. Dolph Ziggler defeated Rusev

2. Jimmy Uso defeated The Miz

3. Charlotte, Paige and Becky Lynch defeated Sasha Banks, Tamina Snuka and Naomi

4. Tyler Breeze defeated Baron Corbin 

5. Dean Ambrose defeated Kevin Owens 

6. Jack Swagger defeated Adam Rose with the Patriot Lock.

7. The New Day defeated The Prime Time Players to retain the WWE Tag Team Championships

8. Seth Rollins defeated John Cena by DQ to retain the WWE World Heavyweight Championship
