Neil Peart has an estimated net worth of $42 million. Peart is a Canadian musician and author who attained most of his wealth as a drummer for the rock band Rush. This musician and author grew up in Port Dalhousie, Ontario.

As an adolescent teen, he joined a few regional bands and moved to the United Kingdom for some time in pursuit of a career as a full-time drummer. After a discouraging stint, he moved back to Canada where he finally joined Toronto's local band Rush in 1974. Peart is also the author of five non-fiction books. Peart made his writing debut with the book titled "The Masked Rider: Cycling in West Africa" and the latest book written by him was released in 2012. In his music career, Peart has received many awards for his musical performances and he is known for his technical proficiency and stamina.
