After 40 Hotel, Nairobi, Kenya

Publish date: 2024-07-12

After 40 Hotel is located right at the heart of Nairobi’s CBD, Biashara Street behind the City Market. 

Our Hotel is primarily focused on providing accommodation to business travelers in Nairobi, Kenya to both local and foreign guests. After40 is a star rated hotel offering accommodation in 63 well-appointed guest rooms. The Hotel is located in Nairobi CBD, Biashara Street behind the City Market and is a value hotel inspired by leading hotels around the world. The hotel offers high comfort accommodation sure to impress even the discerning traveler. After 40 Hotel has a modern and contemporary style promoting a real sense of class.
Guests’ can take pleasure in experiencing the hotel’s perfect blend of modern chic design and its unique décor. The hotel offers an outstanding level of service and attention to detail ensuring our guests’ stay is comfortable and carefree.
The hotel features modern conference rooms that can accommodate from 5 up to 100 participants. The meeting rooms have the latest audio-visual equipment, air conditioning and free high speed, wireless internet access.

The hotel’s location provides excellent access and offers a serene and quiet atmosphere ideal for companies seeking a quiet spot to embark on business seminars, product launches and board room meetings.
