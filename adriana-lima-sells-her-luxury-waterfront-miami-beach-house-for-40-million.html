Adriana Lima Sells Her Luxury Waterfront Miami Beach House For $40 Million

Publish date: 2024-07-13

The supermodel just made a cool profit which is estimated to be millions because she only bought the Miami estate for $9 million in 2010. The property is located on the Biscayne Bay which is situated on America’s Billionaire Bunker, Indian Creek Island, where numerous billionaires owns a property including Ivanka Trump.

The buyer of Lima’s estate is a a British Virgin Islands company called Melep International Ltd, however the true identity of the estate buyer is not revealed to the public. A trust tied to Brazilian model and former Victoria’s Secret Angel’s name paid about $9 million for the four bedroom and four bathroom mansion in 2009. The house is situated on a 6,465 square feet of land which was built in 1958 and could actually be torn down. Lima reportedly hired an interior designer to remodel the home which was completed in 2020.

When Lima bought the mansion situated on the two acre lot on the Billionaire Bunker in 2010 for $9 million, she might have thought about making a profit off of it in time. The Indian Creek which has about 30 acres overlooking water only has 30 mansions in total. The place also has a very tight security and it is an exclusive country club. Lima who currently has a net worth of $95 million has been living on the mansion since 2010 with neighbors like singer-songwriter Julio Iglesias, who apparently owns several parcels of land on the Indian Creek Island. However he reportedly sold one parcel of land in 2020 to Ivanka Trump and Jared Kushner for $30 million.

Numerous celebrities and billionaires continue to buy Miami Beach mansions since 2021 because Miami is the choice location for the artists and celebrities who are looking for a luxury waterfront residence or just a spacious downtown condo in the buildings which are designed by world renowned architects. Victoria Secret veteran selling her Miami mansion on the Indian Creek Island for $40 million just shows how lucrative the market is and it just keeps going at a full throttle. Adriana Lima is a known angel who has wings and the world just love to admire her beauty and charm. The Brazilian model can be considered as one of the most famous former Victoria’s Secret Angels.
