Who Is Janhvi Kapoor?

Janhvi Kapoor is a prominent Indian actress who has made a significant mark in the Bollywood film industry. Born on March 6, 1997, in Mumbai, India, she is the daughter of the iconic actress Sridevi and film producer Boney Kapoor. Janhvi comes from a family deeply rooted in the entertainment industry, with a younger sister, Khushi Kapoor, who is also aspiring to make her mark in the world of cinema.

Janhvi attended the Dhirubhai Ambani International School in Mumbai for her education. Later, she pursued further studies in acting at the renowned Lee Strasberg Theatre & Film Institute in New York, enhancing her skills and preparing for a career in the film industry.

She made her acting debut in 2018 with the romantic drama “Dhadak,” a film that garnered both commercial success and critical acclaim. Janhvi’s performance in portraying a young girl trapped in a challenging marriage was widely appreciated. This debut marked the beginning of her journey as an actress and established her as a promising talent in the industry.

Following “Dhadak,” Janhvi Kapoor has taken on diverse roles in various films, showcasing her versatility as an actress. Some notable projects include the horror anthology “Ghost Stories” (2020), where she displayed her range in the genre, and the biographical drama “Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl” (2020), in which she portrayed the titular character, an Indian Air Force pilot.

Apart from her acting prowess, Janhvi is known for her active presence on social media platforms, particularly Instagram, where she engages with her fans and shares glimpses of her life. With a keen sense of style and a passion for dance, she has garnered a significant following.

Janhvi Kapoor’s journey in the film industry is marked by her dedication to her craft and her ability to take on challenging roles. As the daughter of a Bollywood legend, she continues to navigate the industry with grace, making her own name and contributing to the legacy of her family in Indian cinema.
